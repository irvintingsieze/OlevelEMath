1. Derive the length, area and volume of similar figures

Figures which are similar have the same shape but different sizes.
Below are 2 cylinders which are similar.

Let the diameter(length) of cylinder A and B be $L_A$ and $L_B$ respectively
Let the area of base (shaded in red) for cylinder A and B be $A_A$ and $B_A$ respectively.
Let the volume of cylinder A and B be $V_A$ and $V_B$ respectively.

From the similar figures, we can determine the length / area / volume of the cylinder.
The ratio of areas are = $({{L_A}/{L_B}})^2 = {A_A}/{A_B}$
The ratio of the volume of cylinders = $({{L_A}/{L_B}})^3 = {V_A}/{V_B}$
